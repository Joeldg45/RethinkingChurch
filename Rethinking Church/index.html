Rethinking Church Conference

Rethinking Church is Launch Leadership Network’s flagship conference designed to engender a paradigm shift towards a kingdom-centric dimension of ministry work and also to empower gospel ministers in that direction.

Our Vision

Helping gospel ministers and ministries attain their fullest potentials.

Our Mission

Our mission is indicated by the acronym L.A.U.N.C.H: Learning. Assisting. Upholding. Networking. Challenging. Helping.

Our Network

Our aim is to create an atmosphere where every one called into a leadership position can have access to indepth Learning. We are building a network based Assistance, with the aim of Upholding every member. We are also Networking to foster creative relationships, while Challenging members to establish systems and mechanisms to Help achieve their ministry goals.

Event Speakers

As pastors and leaders, you’re always looking for access to: The latest ideas People with experience Strategies that can help you be more successful at leading your church and ministries and Building a disciple-making church That’s why we’re bringing it all together in one place: Rethinking Church 2020. This year we’re bringing you a lineup of speakers, ideas and strategies that is better than ever before. It’s an opportunity for you to learn, in a small and personal setting, from leaders who have years of experience leading churches, organizations and high-capacity teams.

PHILIP IGBINIJESU

Philip Igbinijesu is the Senior Pastor of Word Assembly with a number of thriving centers across several cities. As a consultant and transformational leadership apostle, his overwhelming passion is to see individuals, families and organizations excel.

OSE SAINT EKOH

Apostle Ose Saint Ekoh is the President of More Ministries and global convener of More Seminars. He is also the lead communicator at More Church, Columbus, Georgia. His mission is to help believers and churches go further and farther. He believes everyone is made for more.

BEN IRABOR

Pastor Ben Irabor is the Lead Pastor of Citadel of Grace Christian Center Abuja. He is actively engaged in the development of tools for ministry that enables church leadership to understand the back doors which births the assimilation of new members and developing e-media platforms that assist Lead Pastors with connecting with their online audience.

GEORGE IRABOR

Pastor George Bryan Irabor is the Lead Pastor of Fountain of Grace Church, Boston. He has developed a standard of excellence that is apparent in his commitment to ministry. His goal is to help build a healthy church and ministry focusing on Church Culture and Systems.

BENNY MOMOH

Benny Momoh is the Lead Pastor of Overcomers Christian Fellowship located in Lithonia, Georgia. He is passionate about connecting people to their life’s purpose by connecting them to God and believes that nothing works without leadership - as such he is committed to training and investing in leaders.

CHRISTIAN OKECHUKWU

Pastor Christian Okechukwu Ebbi is the Lead Pastor of Icon of Grace. He is passionate for the development of excellent and enduring leaders. His drive is to assist ministers in building healthy and relevant churches by developing practical and effective templates for leadership and relationships.

RICH ROBINSON

Rich pioneered Catalyse Change (www.catalysechange.org), that develops training around disciple-making cultures, leadership development processes and authentic & impactful community development. The areas of coaching & consultancy include spiritual formation, disruptive innovation, social entrepreneurship and movemental leadership.
